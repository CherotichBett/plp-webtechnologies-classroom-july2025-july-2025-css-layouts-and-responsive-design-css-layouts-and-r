Welcome

Kenya is often called the “Silicon Savannah”, thanks to its fast-growing tech ecosystem. From innovations like M-Pesa in mobile money to government initiatives such as Konza Technopolis, the country is becoming a hub for digital transformation in Africa.

Strengths

Mobile penetration, fintech success stories, vibrant startup scene.

Opportunities

Growth in agritech, healthtech, edtech, AI, and green tech innovation.

Challenges

Funding access, skills gap, infrastructure gaps, regulatory hurdles.

Quick Facts

  • Nicknamed: Silicon Savannah
  • Flagship project: Konza Technopolis
  • Key sector: Fintech (M-Pesa)
  • Annual ICT growth: ~10%+
